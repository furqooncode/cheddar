
## CHEDDAR LUXURY 
Cheddar is a Nigerian men's luxury streetwear platform built for those who carry themselves different. Browse a curated catalog of premium fits rooted in Nigerian identity and culture, from clean refined pieces to bold statement drops that command attention. Pay seamlessly and securely with Paystack, with a checkout experience as smooth as the fits you're copping. Every order is tracked from the moment you check out to the moment it lands at your door. Fast, clean, and built for the man who doesn't settle. Cheddar isn't just a store, it's a standard. Nigerian luxury streetwear, done right. **Wear Nigerian. Move different.**
